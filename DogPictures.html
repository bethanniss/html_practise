A Basset Hound

This is Harvey the Basset Hound. The Basset Hound’s powerful sense of smell is second only to the Bloodhound’s

A Miniature Pinscher

This is Monty the Miniature Pinscher. They were origially developed in Germany to kill rats in homes and stables, and are not related to the Doberman

A Jackapoo

This is Ruby the Jackapoo. Jackapoos are not a recognised breed by The Kennel Club, but are a hybrid of a Toy Poodle and a Jack Russel . They have inherited intelligence from both lineages, and I think they will grow in popularity.

A Rescue

This is Scout, he is rescue and a mix of breeds. Of the 6.5 million animals that enter animal shelters nationwide every year, 3.3 million of those are dogs.
